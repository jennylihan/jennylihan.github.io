Coming Home: High School Workshops

My friend and fellow Stanford student Cindy Nguyen are proud alumni of public schools in the bay area.

Freshman year, we felt the urge to take some of the immmense resources we found at Stanford and bring them back to our communities.

With funding from the Haas Center for Public Service, we started an initiative, Stanford Catalist, devoted to promoting college access at Independence High, Cindy's alma mater. Some activities we hosted included: a free mock SAT practice test, guest speakers from different majors, college application advice, free trips to Stanford (organized by Stanford Splash).
