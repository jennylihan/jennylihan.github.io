About Me

Hi! I'm a third year undergraduate at Stanford University studying Symbolic Systems (essentially, cognitive science with a computational focus) and hopefully a minor in Spanish. My interests include educational equity, human-centered design, cross-cultural collaboration, science communication, and technology for social good.

In five years, you'll probably find me (1) in a classroom somewhere around the world as a high school science educator or (2) at an edtech start up grappling with constructionist pedagogy and software bugs.

In my free time, I'm biking, volunteering, Wikipedia spiraling, contemplating the universe, or exploring megacities.
